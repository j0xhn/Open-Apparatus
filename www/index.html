FAQ


Q: How does the Open Apparatus work?
It's like a secret society for anyone that has a dream.... except it isn't secret. Click here to download our info packet.

Q: Does it cost to join?
Depends what you want, but typically no. If you are willing to give back to the community then all we ask is that you commit to working towards your dream. That is payment enough. But if you want to do your own thing then we offer our services of branding, web development, and other tasks at very resonable prices.

Q: What kind of projects or people are most likely to fit into the OA?
Basically we aren't some sort of super exclusive group or anything like that. But you will recieve the most help if it is the kind of idea that even if it never makes any money at all, everyone involved will still feel their time was well spent working on it. These kinds of projects usually fall into A) super cool artsy projects and/or B) projects that make the world a better place.

Q: How do you make money then?
Funny you ask. We sell our services to and freelance on the side. Some have other means of finance, as well as a store where we have a couple of items that get purchased. Buy something and help support the OA.

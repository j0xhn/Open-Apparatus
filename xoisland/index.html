Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real?

Absolutely. We saw this blog post and decided to take it to the next level. At first it may seem a little far fetched, but it makes a lot of sense and the more you learn about it the more you'll love it.

Are the packages above the only ones?

We offer packages for nearly any interested group size. This includes multiple payment options and in-between packages. Send your questions to info@xoisland.com.

Where is the Island located

Located near Cayo Grape in the Caribbean, XO Island can be accessed from a town called "Bluefields". Here is a link to the google map. To see the exact group of islands you have to download google earth though.

What Does Unlimited Access Imply?

It means that if your package hasn't been reserved two weeks in advance you are free to reserve it for as long as you wish. This comes with the annual membership fee of $90/person (which is discounted with group packages)

Have you been to the Island?

Not yet. We've spoken with the realator and plan on going down in April. Want to come? John and Bryson speak Spanish fluently, and have visited every country in Central America and have a good feel for the culture.

Why the yearly fee?

The up-front cost is in putting a down payment on the island. We need annual payments for paying off the mortgage, taxes, maintenance, gas, water, internet, etc...
